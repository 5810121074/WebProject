Multi-Hip

Hip Flexion

1

Strengthen the muscles running along the front of your thighs, which flex your hips,

moving your legs forward and away from your body. Stand with your right hip toward the weight stack and the thigh pad in its lowest position so it is resting in the middle of the front of your right thigh.

2

Grasp the handles to stabilize your upper body and maintain your balance.

3

Lift your right knee up toward the ceiling as if you are marching with your right leg.

Hold this position for two to three seconds, and then lower it back down. Repeat 10 times and work up to three sets. Reverse your position and repeat the exercise to strengthen your left leg.
